Rizal Memorial Library and Museum

The Rizal Memorial Library and Museum is a neoclassical landmark located in Cebu City, dedicated to preserving the region’s rich historical and cultural legacy.

Named in honor of national hero Dr. José Rizal, the building houses a public library on the ground floor and a museum on the upper level. The museum features a collection of Cebuano artworks, historical photographs, cultural relics, and memorabilia that reflect the local identity and heritage. It also serves as a venue for art exhibits and cultural events, making it an important center for learning and artistic expression in the heart of the city.
