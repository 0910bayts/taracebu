D' FAMILY PARK

D' Family Park is a 20-hectare nature and recreation park located in Nasipit, Talamban, Cebu City.

Managed by the Metropolitan Cebu Water District (MCWD), it was inaugurated on May 8, 1999, with the aim of providing a wholesome,

family-friendly environment while promoting environmental conservation through its diverse flora and fauna.
