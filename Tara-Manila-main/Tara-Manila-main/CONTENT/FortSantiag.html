TOPS Cebu

Tops Lookout, commonly known as Tops Cebu, is a renowned hilltop destination situated in the Busay Hills of Cebu City.

Perched approximately 600 meters above sea level, it offers panoramic vistas of Cebu City, Mandaue, Mactan Island, and even the distant Bohol coastline.
