CEBU SAFARI AND ADVENTURE PARK

Cebu Safari and Adventure Park is the largest zoological park in the Philippines, spanning 170 hectares in the mountainous

area of Barangay Corte, Carmen, Cebu. Established in 2018 by Michel J. Lhuillier, this premier safari destination houses over

1,000 animals representing approximately 120 species from around the globe.
